Mada Game Earn

About Us

Mada Game Earn is Malawi's free Play & Earn web portal, built in Lilongwe.

We offer simple HTML5 arcade games for entertainment. Users aged 18+ can play games, complete optional offers, and collect coins. Coins can be redeemed for small MWK airtime rewards on Fridays.

We are NOT gambling, betting, or an investment app. No deposit is required. Rewards are funded by advertising partners.

Our mission: Give Malawian youth fun, free games while learning about the digital economy.

Launched: May 2026
Location: Lilongwe, Central Region, Malawi
