Quick summary: We only collect what we need to pay you and keep the app fair. We collect your email/phone for payouts, your device ID for fraud prevention, and survey data via TheoremReach. We never sell your personal data.

Overview

Mada Game Earn ("we", "us") is a free-to-play rewards app for users in Malawi. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and your choices. By using the app, you agree to this policy. We operate in accordance with the Malawi Data Protection Act and related consumer protection principles.

1. Information We Collect

We minimize data collection to only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Contact for Payouts — Email and/or Phone Number: Required to process your earnings via Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, or other supported methods, send payout confirmations, and recover your account.
  • Device Information — Device ID, IP Address, OS Version: Used for fraud prevention, preventing duplicate or fake accounts, ensuring fair play, and troubleshooting.
  • App Activity: Points earned, tasks completed, games played, redemption history, and referral data to operate your wallet.
  • Survey Data (via TheoremReach): When you voluntarily choose to complete surveys, TheoremReach may ask for demographic information (e.g., age range, gender, region, household info) to match you with relevant surveys. You provide this directly to them. We receive only a confirmation of completion and reward amount, not your individual answers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and secure your account
  • To credit points and process withdrawals to your phone or email
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse
  • To improve the app performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations in Malawi

3. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted partners to provide surveys and offers. They operate under their own privacy policies:

TheoremReach
Survey provider
AdGem
Offerwall provider

We share only the minimum data needed with them (such as device ID or advertising ID) to attribute rewards and prevent fraud. We do not control their data collection.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Our app and website use localStorage and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, save your progress, and prevent fraud. We do not use third-party advertising cookies to track you across other apps. You can clear app data in your device settings, but this will log you out.

5. Data Sharing and Selling

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

We only share data in these limited cases:

  • With mobile money providers to send your payout
  • With TheoremReach and AdGem to credit your survey/offer rewards
  • If required by Malawian law, court order, or to protect against fraud

6. Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep your information safe. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, and for up to 24 months after closure to prevent fraud and meet legal requirements. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct — update your email or phone number
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent — stop using the app and uninstall at any time

To exercise these rights, email us from the address linked to your account. We will respond within 14 days.

Children's Privacy

Mada Game Earn is not intended for children under 13 years old (or under 18 without parental consent). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us for deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. We will update the "Last updated" date and notify you in-app for significant changes.
